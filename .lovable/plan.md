

# Isioalo Car Wash Website

A modern, professional website for Isioalo Car Wash with the tagline "Your Car Is An Extension Of Your Image." Clean design with a blue/red/white color scheme inspired by the business branding, featuring high-quality stock-style car wash imagery of luxury vehicles (BMW, Mercedes-Benz, etc.) using free Unsplash/Pexels URLs.

## Pages

### Home (/)
- **Hero section**: Full-width background image of a luxury car being washed, bold headline "ISIOALO CAR WASH", tagline, and CTA button to view services
- **Quick highlights**: 3 cards — Affordable Prices, Premium Service, Open 7 Days
- **Operating hours banner**: 07:30 – 18:00
- **Contact strip**: Phone numbers for Melusi (063 433 9510) and Peter (061 504 0592)

### Services (/services)
- **Cars pricing table**: Wash & Go R30, Wash & Dry R40, Full Wash R80, Executive Wash R100
- **SUVs pricing table**: Wash & Go R40, Wash & Dry R50, Full Wash R90, Executive Wash R110
- **Extras**: Engine Wash R60, Vacuum R30, Polish R20, Valet – Subject to Enquiry
- Each section with a relevant luxury car image

### Contact (/contact)
- Contact details with phone icons for Melusi & Peter
- Operating hours
- WhatsApp call-to-action buttons
- "Refreshments Available" note

## Design
- **Color palette**: Deep blue primary, red accents, white backgrounds — matching the physical signage
- **Typography**: Bold, clean headings; modern body text
- **Imagery**: Unsplash URLs of luxury cars (Mercedes, BMW) at car washes — water droplets, shiny finishes
- **Shared header/footer** across all pages with navigation and contact info
